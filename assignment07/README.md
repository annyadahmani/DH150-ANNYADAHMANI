### Tasks
* Task 1 Filter: The filter feature allows for users to personalize their searches by filtering for things they are looking for in a recipe and filter out things they do not want in a recipe.
* Task 2 Social Media Feed: The social media feed feature allows users to interact with their friends and famous people they follow to see their posts and new recipes they've created. Users are also able to comment, like, dislike,  and share other user's posts and recipes. Users are also able to post their own recipes to their feed for their friends and followers to see
* Task 3 Review: The review feature allows for users to see other's recipes and see reviews that people left on their  recipe. User's are also able to leave their own reviews on the recipe.
* Task 4 Sort: The sort by feature allows users to sort the recipes they are looking at in a certain way, which allows users the power to see what recipes they see first and last in a list.
